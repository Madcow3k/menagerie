Delete Your Account

From the App

You can delete your account directly in the app. Go to Settings, scroll to the bottom, and tap "Delete Account." The app will walk you through a confirmation before anything is removed.

By Email

If you can't access the app, send an email to hello@mymenagerie.pet from the email address associated with your account. Include "Delete my account" in the subject line. We'll process your request within 7 days.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile and login credentials
  • All animals and their care logs
  • Photos you uploaded
  • Terrarium configurations
  • Sensor integrations and historical readings
  • Household and member associations

What We Keep

If you contributed care data while your account was active, anonymized and aggregated records may be retained as part of our research dataset. This data cannot be traced back to you or your animals. No personal information is kept after deletion.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel active subscriptions. Please cancel your subscription through the App Store or Google Play before deleting your account to avoid future charges.
