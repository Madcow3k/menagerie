Where we're headed

The vision is
bigger than an app.

Menagerie starts as your personal care tracker and grows into something much bigger — a platform connecting keepers, breeders, vets, and researchers around the animals we all love.

Phase 1 — Launch (March 2026)

  • Animal profiles with species-specific defaults
  • Color-coded environment monitoring & alerts
  • Feeding and weight tracking with trend charts
  • Infirmary system for medical records
  • Multi-user household & petsitter access
  • iOS and Android native apps

Phase 2 — Growth (Mid 2026)

  • WiFi sensor automation (Govee, SwitchBot)
  • Breeding records and morph/genetics tracking
  • Inventory management with barcode scanning
  • QR code enclosure labels
  • CSV & PDF data export
  • Push notifications and care reminders

Phase 3 — Community (2026–2027)

  • AI-powered care recommendations
  • Veterinary EMR export and direct sharing
  • Exotic vet clinic portal
  • Opt-in research data contributions
  • University and conservation partnerships

Phase 4 — Industry (Future)

  • Commercial breeder & facility management
  • Species population data for conservation
  • API access for researchers & institutions
  • Marketplace integrations for trusted suppliers
  • Industry-wide care standards dataset
